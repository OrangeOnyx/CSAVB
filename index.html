How It Works

From Sign-Up to Game Day

Four simple steps to get your athlete on the court.

1
Register Online

Visit cajunsportsvolleyball.com, select your child's grade, and complete registration. Jersey included.

2
Get Placed on a Team

Every registered player is placed on a team. No tryouts. No cuts. Equal playing time guaranteed.

3
Weekly Practice

One practice per week at Comeaux Sports Complex. Coaches are background-checked and SafeSport certified.

4
Weekend Games

Weekend matches at Earl K. Long Gym (ULL campus). Season ends with a tournament. 9 weeks total.

All CSA Volleyball activities take place at two facilities in Lafayette, Louisiana.

Practice Facility

Weekly practices are held here throughout the season. Arrive no more than 15 minutes early. Pick up within 15 minutes of end time.

Rules & Equipment

What You Need to Know

Simple rules, clear expectations, and minimal gear requirements to get your athlete on the court.

🎽 Equipment
  • Jersey included with registration
  • Athletic shorts (player-provided)
  • Non-marking court shoes required
  • Knee pads strongly recommended
  • No jewelry during play
  • Hair must be pulled back
🏐 Match Format
  • Best 2 of 3 sets format
  • Sets 1 & 2 played to 25 points
  • Set 3 (if needed) played to 15
  • Rally scoring throughout
  • 5-in-a-row rule in effect
  • Net heights vary by division
👥 Coaching Standards
  • All coaches must be 18 or older
  • Background check required
  • SafeSport training required
  • Equal playing time enforced
  • No-cut policy — all players placed
  • Positive coaching environment
